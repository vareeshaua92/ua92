Our Artists

Coi Leray

Coi Leray Collins, an American rapper and singer, was born on May 11, 1997. In 2018, she started sharing songs on SoundCloud and published her debut mixtape Everythingcoz and the track "Huddy." Leray released her second mixtape, EC2 (2019), and her debut extended play, Now or Never, following the signing of a record deal with The Music Label (2022).

Big Sean

Big Sean is a stage name for rapper Sean Michael Leonard Anderson, who was born in America on March 25, 1988. With the release of his third mixtape, Eventually Famous Vol. 3: Huge, in 2010, Anderson, who started his music career in 2007, became more well-known. The Music Label then offered him a recording deal.

Lizzo

Lizzo is the stage name of Melissa Viviane Jefferson, an American singer, rapper, and flautist born on April 27, 1988. She was born in Detroit, Michigan, and when she was ten years old, her family relocated to Houston. She relocated to Minneapolis after graduating from college, where she started a recording career in hip-hop music.

Chris Brown

Christopher Maurice Brown, an American singer, songwriter, dancer, and actor, was born on May 5, 1989. Widely known as the "King of R&B.".

Drake

A Canadian rapper, singer, and actor by the name of Aubrey Drake Graham was born on October 24, 1986. Drake, a significant personality in modern popular music, is credited with spreading the popularity of singing and R&B sensibility in hip hop.
